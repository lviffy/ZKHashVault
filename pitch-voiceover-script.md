# ZKHashVault Pitch Voice-Over Script

## Quick Recording Setup for ElevenLabs
- Voice style: Confident, clear, founder-style
- Pace: Medium (about 1.0x, slight pauses between sections)
- Tone: Trustworthy, technical but simple
- Target length: 2:55 to 3:10
- Estimated script length: ~410 to 430 words

## Screen-by-Screen Script

### Scene 1 (0:00 - 0:10)
**Screen:** Browser tab and homepage opening

**Say:**
Welcome to ZKHashVault, an AI-assisted yield vault where every major allocation decision is bounded by cryptographic safety checks directly on-chain.

---

### Scene 2 (0:10 - 0:26)
**Screen:** Landing page hero section with headline and CTA buttons

**Say:**
Traditional vaults ask users to trust that risk is being managed correctly behind the scenes. ZKHashVault replaces trust with verification. Our system continuously balances yield and risk, and enforces safety constraints before capital is allowed to move.

---

### Scene 3 (0:26 - 0:44)
**Screen:** Scroll to Protocol Features cards

**Say:**
The protocol is built on four pillars: zero-knowledge verified state, on-chain credit scoring, adaptive AI rebalancing, and strict smart contract guardrails. Together, they create an autonomous vault designed to pursue yield while preserving predictable risk boundaries.

---

### Scene 4 (0:44 - 0:52)
**Screen:** Click Launch App or Open Console

**Say:**
Now we jump into the live strategy console to see this system in action.

---

### Scene 5 (0:52 - 1:08)
**Screen:** Dashboard top nav showing Live Engine, network, wallet connect area

**Say:**
At the top, users instantly see live engine status, connected network, and wallet state. This creates execution clarity: what chain we are on, whether automation is active, and who controls the position.

---

### Scene 6 (1:08 - 1:30)
**Screen:** Strategy Overview section and My Position card

**Say:**
On the left is My Position, where deposit and withdrawal are handled in one clean flow. Users can see vault balance, current earnings, and a live credit profile. As account behavior improves over time, stronger credit quality can support more efficient capital utilization.

---

### Scene 7 (1:30 - 1:44)
**Screen:** Expected APY and Risk Class cards

**Say:**
The strategy engine continuously updates expected net APY and current risk class. Users get immediate visibility into both reward potential and present risk posture, in plain language.

---

### Scene 8 (1:44 - 1:58)
**Screen:** Active Strategy Insight panel

**Say:**
The Active Strategy Insight explains why capital is positioned this way right now, using volatility, utilization, slippage thresholds, and health factor constraints as decision inputs.

**Optional alternate line if a rejection state appears:**
If an AI recommendation violates policy, it is blocked automatically. Safety rules are enforced by contracts, not by operator discretion.

---

### Scene 9 (1:58 - 2:16)
**Screen:** AI Strategy Assistant chat panel

**Say:**
Users can also talk to the AI strategy assistant directly. Instead of decoding raw protocol internals, they can ask why a position changed, request adjustments, and receive clear explanations of the current strategy.
Typical prompts include: Why did APY move today? What changes if I switch to a more conservative risk profile? Compare my current allocation to Aave or Compound alternatives. Explain this rebalance in simple terms. And, show me the safety checks before execution.

**Optional on-screen prompt bubbles during this scene:**
- Why did my allocation change this hour?
- How much yield am I giving up if I lower risk one level?
- What is my current health factor and liquidation buffer?
- Simulate a 20% market dip on my current position.
- Explain this rebalance as if I am new to DeFi.

---

### Scene 10 (2:16 - 2:32)
**Screen:** Gemini AI Target Allocation section with pool bars and reasoning

**Say:**
Allocation recommendations are visualized across pools with percentages and rationale. This converts opaque model output into understandable portfolio intent that users can inspect before action.

---

### Scene 11 (2:32 - 2:48)
**Screen:** Cryptographic Security panel and Verify Safety Proof button

**Say:**
This section is the trust anchor of the product. Before sensitive actions proceed, zero-knowledge proofs validate that risk boundaries remain intact. With one click, users can submit verification on-chain and independently confirm safety guarantees.

---

### Scene 12 (2:48 - 3:00)
**Screen:** On-Chain Rebalance History list with explorer links

**Say:**
Every rebalance is transparent and auditable with explorer-linked history. ZKHashVault combines autonomous yield optimization, AI-driven decisioning, and cryptographic enforcement into one verifiable vault experience. This is programmable asset management built for accountable risk.

---
