# ZKHashVault Pitch Voice-Over Script

## Quick Recording Setup for ElevenLabs
- Voice style: Confident, clear, founder-style
- Pace: Medium (about 0.95x to 1.0x)
- Tone: Trustworthy, technical but simple
- Target length: 2.5 to 3.5 minutes

## Screen-by-Screen Script

### Scene 1 (0:00 - 0:10)
**Screen:** Browser tab and homepage opening

**Say:**
Welcome to ZKHashVault, a smart yield vault where every allocation decision is protected by cryptographic safety checks on-chain.

---

### Scene 2 (0:10 - 0:30)
**Screen:** Landing page hero section with headline and CTA buttons

**Say:**
Most vaults ask users to trust off-chain risk management. ZKHashVault replaces trust with proof. Our system continuously balances yield and risk, and verifies safety constraints before capital moves.

---

### Scene 3 (0:30 - 0:55)
**Screen:** Scroll to Protocol Features cards

**Say:**
At the core are four pillars: zero-knowledge verified state, on-chain credit scoring, adaptive AI rebalancing, and smart contract execution guardrails. Together, they create an autonomous vault that is both profitable and safety-first.

---

### Scene 4 (0:55 - 1:05)
**Screen:** Click Launch App or Open Console

**Say:**
Now let us open the live strategy console.

---

### Scene 5 (1:05 - 1:25)
**Screen:** Dashboard top nav showing Live Engine, network, wallet connect area

**Say:**
This is the live dashboard on HashKey Testnet. The engine status, network context, and wallet connection are visible at the top so users always know where execution is happening.

---

### Scene 6 (1:25 - 1:50)
**Screen:** Strategy Overview section and My Position card

**Say:**
On the left is My Position, where users can deposit or withdraw in one flow. The card surfaces vault balance, earnings, and a credit score profile. As user behavior improves, credit quality can influence better capital efficiency.

---

### Scene 7 (1:50 - 2:05)
**Screen:** Expected APY and Risk Class cards

**Say:**
In real time, the strategy engine computes expected net APY and a current risk class. This gives users immediate visibility into reward potential and risk posture.

---

### Scene 8 (2:05 - 2:25)
**Screen:** Active Strategy Insight panel

**Say:**
The strategy insight explains why capital is positioned this way right now, based on volatility, utilization, slippage limits, and health factor constraints.

**Optional alternate line if a rejection state appears:**
If a recommendation violates policy, execution is blocked automatically. Safety rules are enforced by contracts, not by promise.

---

### Scene 9 (2:25 - 2:50)
**Screen:** AI Strategy Assistant chat panel

**Say:**
Users can also interact with the AI strategy assistant directly to understand positioning, ask for adjustments, and get plain-language guidance instead of raw protocol complexity.

---

### Scene 10 (2:50 - 3:15)
**Screen:** Gemini AI Target Allocation section with pool bars and reasoning

**Say:**
Here we visualize the AI recommendation across pools. Instead of opaque output, users see allocation percentages and concise reasoning behind each move.

---

### Scene 11 (3:15 - 3:40)
**Screen:** Cryptographic Security panel and Verify Safety Proof button

**Say:**
This is the trust anchor of the product. Before sensitive actions proceed, zero-knowledge proofs validate that risk boundaries remain intact. With one click, users can submit proof verification on-chain and independently confirm system safety.

---

### Scene 12 (3:40 - 4:00)
**Screen:** On-Chain Rebalance History list with explorer links

**Say:**
Every rebalance is transparent and auditable. Users can inspect historical reallocations, health factors, and transaction evidence directly on the explorer.

---

### Scene 13 (4:00 - 4:20)
**Screen:** Zoom out to full dashboard and brand mark

**Say:**
ZKHashVault combines autonomous yield optimization, AI-driven decisioning, and cryptographic enforcement into one verifiable vault experience. This is what on-chain asset management looks like when safety is programmable.

---

## Short Closing Line Options
- ZKHashVault: smarter yield, provable safety.
- From AI signal to on-chain proof, every step is accountable.
